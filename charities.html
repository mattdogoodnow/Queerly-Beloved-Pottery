charities

two organizations doing work that i admire

it's my personal commitment to donate as much as i can to these incredible organizations working to protect and empower LGBTQIA2S+ youth of faith.

to date: $2,254.06 donated

Born Perfect, a program launched by the National Center for Lesbian Rights in 2014, is dedicated to ending conversion therapy. Through legislative advocacy, legal action, and public awareness, Born Perfect works to protect LGBTQ+ youth from these dangerous practices. The program's mission is to ensure that every LGBTQ+ child is recognized as "Born Perfect," with their identity honored and protected from stigma and harm.

Beloved Arise is the first national organization dedicated to empowering LGBTQIA+ youth to embrace both their faith and queer identity. As a multi-faith community, Beloved Arise celebrates and supports queer youth and young adults from all spiritual traditions, uplifting them in their diverse identities, expressions, and aspirations.
